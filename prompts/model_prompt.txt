MODEL VISUALIZATION RULES

Jewelry is the hero. Model is secondary.
Use Pakistani / South Asian / Middle Eastern luxury styling.
Use modest formal attire only. No deep neckline. No revealing styling.
Preserve exact product design, scale, polish, stone shapes, stone count, motif and construction.

Close-up means:
- product must be fully visible
- jewelry should occupy strong visual focus
- face should not overpower jewelry
- no cropping of jewelry

Ring rules:
- one ring on one finger only
- never span two fingers
- preserve center stone cut: marquise, pear, oval, emerald, round, princess etc.
- preserve bypass/cross band direction
- preserve rose/white/yellow/Ganga Jamni polish
- no generic solitaire conversion

Bangle/bracelet rules:
- show on wrist
- preserve thickness, width and repeat pattern
- preserve leaf/chevron/floral/zircon setting

Necklace rules:
- preserve strand count
- preserve pendant/drop count
- preserve full necklace curve

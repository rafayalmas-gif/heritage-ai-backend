--------------------------------------------------
IMAGE QUALITY CHECK
--------------------------------------------------

Before generation determine confidence.

If image contains:

• fingers covering jewelry
• blur
• reflections
• screenshot UI
• heavy perspective distortion
• cropped product
• missing areas
• insufficient resolution

AND confidence is below 98%

DO NOT GENERATE.

Ask user for:

• cleaner image
OR
• another angle
OR
• higher resolution photo

Accuracy is more important than completion.

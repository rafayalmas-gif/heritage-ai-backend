STONE EDIT RULES

If mixed stone zones exist, ask first:
1. Main / large center stones only
2. Side stones only
3. All colored stones
4. Drops / hanging stones
5. Pearls only
6. Custom

Preserve:
stone shape, stone size, stone count, prongs, setting, bezels, white zircon/diamond halos, metal polish, motif and background.

Do not enlarge stones.
Do not thicken prongs.
Do not redesign side patterns.
